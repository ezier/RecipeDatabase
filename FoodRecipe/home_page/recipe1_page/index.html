Thai Soup Thumb

  • Prepare Time: 15 Min
  • Cook Time: 10 Min
  • Calories: 310
  • Difficulty: medium
  • Gluten-free: No
  • Rate:




SLICE
Thinly slice the chicken breasts crosswise into strips.

BOIL
Heat the vegetable oil in a medium pot over medium-high heat. Add the onion and red pepper. Cook until softened, stirring occasionally, about 2 minutes. Add the garlic, ginger and curry paste and cook for 30 seconds. Add the chicken broth, coconut milk and brown sugar. Cover and bring to a boil.

SIMMER
Add the chicken and simmer until cooked through, about 5 to 7 minutes. The chicken is done when it feels firm to the touch and a meat thermometer inserted in the center reaches 170Â°F.

SERVE
Remove from heat and stir in the lime juice, cilantro and zucchini noodles. Serve with lime wedges and Sriracha sauce.

TA DA!

Chicken Breasts
4

Chicken Broth
4 cups

Brown Sugar
1 tbsp.

Limes
2
